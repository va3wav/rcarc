GET LICENSED

Getting licensed for Amateur Radio is a easy as one 100 multiple choice question exam. Scoring 70% gets you a Basic license with use of frequencies above 30mhz and scoring 80% or more gets you Basic with Honours and the use of the full Amateur Radio frequency bands in Canada.

If you are ready to take an exam in the Renfrew County area, contact our Examiner:
Steve White VA3WAV

If you want to know how to study for the exam, take a look at the great resources listed on this site:
lid.radio/ham-study-stuff/

The regulatory body ISED also has practice exams you can take any time.
