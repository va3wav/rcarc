WELCOME!

Thanks for checking out the Renfrew County Amateur Radio Club. We are a group of radio amateurs located in Renfrew County who meet regularly to discuss all aspects of the hobby. We have members interested in contesting, homebrew, digital modes, APRS, repeater building, pico balloons, Winlink, emergency communications and lots more!
